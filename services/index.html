Our Services

We do Software Engineering, Technology Consulting and Individual as well as Group Training.

Software Engineering

simplabs builds solutions for the web and mobile devices for everyone from small startups to big enterprises. Our solutions are well architected and easy to maintain and extend by our customers once we're gone.

We turn our customer's ideas into successful products by taking over projects completely or augmenting existing teams to help reach delivery deadlines or improve quality.

Consulting

We also offer consulting services to help clients successfully implement their own projects. Having years of experience with Ruby on Rails, Ember.js, REST interfaces and scaling of large systems, we can help find performance bottlenecks in existing applications, review architectures or design complex client server systems.

Training

simplabs offers individual and group training by tutors with extensive experience in the respective technologies. From introductory workshops on frameworks like Ruby on Rails or Ember.js to advanced classes on REST API design, authentication, deployment or scalability, we can set up coaching sessions that suit the needs of clients and their teams.
