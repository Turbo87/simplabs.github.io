About

Engineering Agency based in Munich, Germany

simplabs is an Engineering and Consulting Agency based in Munich, Germany. It was founded by Marco Otte-Witte in 2014 after he had worked freelance for numerous startups for over 10 years. simplabs offers Engineering, Consulting and Training with web and mobile technologies. If we cannot provide something ourselves, we have a network of trusted engineers and designers that we work with.


simplabs Open Source

simplabs loves Open Source and we don't only use it to implement our customer's projects but also actively contribute and maintain a number of projects ourselves; you might have stumbled upon the Ember.js authentication library Ember Simple Auth for example. Even the source for this website is available on github.

We engage in the Community

simplabs is a founding member of BITS:connect, the Bavarian Internet Technology Startups association. We also sponsor and organize the Munich Ember.js Meetup - would be great to see you at the next meeting!
