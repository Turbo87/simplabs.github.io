Engineering Agency based in Munich, Germany

simplabs is an Engineering and Consulting agency based in Munich, Germany. It was founded by Marco Otte-Witte after he had worked for numerous startups and other kinds of technology companies for over 10 years. simplabs has a network of trusted freelance engineers and designers that we work with if we cannot do something ourselves.

simplabs Open Source

simplabs loves Open Source and we don't only use it to realize our customer's projects but also actively contribute and maintain a number of projects ourselves. You might e.g. have stumbled upon the Ember.js authentication library Ember Simple Auth. You can also find the source for this website on github.

The Community

simplabs engages in the community as well. Besides giving talks e.g. on the Munich Ruby on Rails user group we also sponsor and organize the Munich Ember.js Meetup.
