Engineering Agency based in Munich, Germany

simplabs is an Engineering and Consulting Agency based in Munich, Germany. It was founded by Marco Otte-Witte after having worked freelance for numerous startups for over 10 years. If we cannot provide something ourselves, we have a network of trusted freelance engineers and designers that we work with.

simplabs Open Source

simplabs loves Open Source and we don't only use it to realize our customer's projects but also actively contribute and maintain a number of projects ourselves. You might e.g. have stumbled upon the Ember.js authentication library Ember Simple Auth. You can also find the source for this website on github.

We also sponsor and organize the Munich Ember.js Meetup - would be great to see you at the next meetup!
