Our Process

We are enthusiastic about agile practices and a flexible development process. While we don't think planning is evil per se we don't overplan and communicate with our customers a lot. We usually recommend having a kick-off workshop to get the project going and from then on refine things as needed. As a project is implemented we will not only regularly communicate progress but also the how and why behind design decisions we make.
