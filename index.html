Solutions for the web and mobile

simplabs builds solutions for the web and mobile devices that are well architected and easy to maintain and extend. We focus on Ruby on Rails, Ember.js and Swift but will happily use other technology stacks that are suited to get the job done. We follow agile methodologies and communicate with our customers frequently.

Consulting and Coaching

We also offer individual and group training. If you want to improve e.g. your Ruby on Rails or Ember.js skills, contact us and we'll set up a workshop. simplabs will also review your application architecture, help you find performance bottlenecks or assist with big decisions that you don't feel comfortable to make on your own.

Open Source

We believe in open source. Besides using it in our projects we also contribute back with projects like Ember.SimpleAuth or Excellent. Check out all of the projects on github and get involved! simplabs also organizes and sponsors the Munich Ember.js Meetup - see you there!
